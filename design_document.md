# Abazabet Design Document

## 1. Overview

This document outlines the design and features for Abazabet, a full-stack online betting platform. The platform will be inspired by popular betting websites like 1xbet, offering a comprehensive and engaging user experience.

## 2. Key Features

Based on the initial research, the following features are essential for a competitive betting website:

*   **User Account Management:** Secure user registration, login, and profile management.
*   **Sports Betting:** A wide range of sports and events to bet on, including live betting.
*   **Casino Games:** A selection of popular casino games like slots, blackjack, and roulette.
*   **Payment Integration:** Secure and reliable deposit and withdrawal options, including cryptocurrencies.
*   **Promotions and Bonuses:** Attractive bonuses and promotions to attract and retain users.
*   **Live Streaming:** Live streaming of sports events to enhance the betting experience.
*   **Virtual Sports:** AI-created sporting events for users to bet on.
*   **Responsive Design:** A mobile-friendly design that works seamlessly on all devices.

## 3. Design and UI/UX

The design will be modern, clean, and intuitive. The user interface will be easy to navigate, allowing users to quickly find the events and games they are interested in. The color scheme will be professional and engaging, with a focus on usability and accessibility.

## 4. Technology Stack

*   **Frontend:** React
*   **Backend:** Flask (Python)
*   **Database:** PostgreSQL

## 5. Next Steps

The next step is to start developing the backend and creating the necessary APIs. This will be followed by the frontend development and UI implementation. Finally, the platform will be thoroughly tested and deployed.
